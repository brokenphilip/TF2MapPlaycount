TF2 Map Playcount v1.0

For more info (or giving suggestions/reporting bugs), click here.

Attach the following files one-by-one (using the "Choose File" option above, or by drag & drop):

  • viewed.res (from "Team Fortress 2\tf\media") - shows times played (official and community maps)
  • tf2_playerstats.dmx (from "Team Fortress 2\tf") - shows time spent (for official maps only)

No data is sent anywhere - your browser does all the work, locally on your machine.

Some data may be wrong or missing - unfortunately, this is a TF2 limitation.

If you wish to reattach the files and update your data, you must reset it first.

"Time per match" will be calculated when both files have been attached. Times are formatted in H:MM:SS.
